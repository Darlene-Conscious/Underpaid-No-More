Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to leave my job right away?

No. This kit is designed so you can build your business foundation while still employed. Many women start this work quietly on the side until they're ready to transition.

What if I don't know what kind of business I want yet?

That's exactly what Module 2 helps you figure out. We map your existing skills to problems people will pay to solve — you don't need to come in with a business idea already formed.

Do I need tech skills or social media?

No. This system focuses on simple, practical methods to find your first clients without needing advanced tech or a large social media following.

Is this only for women?

This kit is centred on women's experiences of systemic underpayment, but it's also for anyone in undervalued roles — including men in feminine-coded work — and for partners who want to understand and support someone who's been impacted.

How much time will this take?

It's broken into small, manageable steps. You can move through it at your own pace — whether that's a few hours a week or more intensive focus. The key is consistent progress, not speed.
